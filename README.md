# dad-mod

An evidence-based, interactive weight-training course — built to take someone from a standing start to
confident, structured training for **muscle and metabolic health**, with every claim cited to meta-analyses and
primary physiology.

**▶ Live course: https://chrisfraser.github.io/dad-mod/**

## What's inside

A self-paced course of interactive HTML lessons, each with something to drag, toggle, or play with.

**Phase 1 — Foundations (the science)**
1. The Big Rocks — the six levers that drive ~90% of results, and honest goals
2. How Muscle Actually Grows — tension → growth, and why early "gains" are swelling
3. Levers, Tension & Your Wrist — torque, moment arms, and a wrist-sparing approach
4. Muscle, the Glucose Sink — how training improves metabolic health

**Phase 2 — The Lifts (form)**
5. The Hinge & the Squat · 6. Press & Pull, Wrist-Safe · 7. Calibrating Effort (RIR)

**Phase 3 — Fuel & Recover**
8. Protein Without Obsession · 9. Creatine & the Evidence-Based Shelf · 10. Sleep, Recovery & the Small Deficit

**Phase 4 — Keep Progressing** *(in progress)*

Plus a printable [Program Card](reference/program-card.html) and a [Glossary](reference/glossary.html).

## How it's built

Plain HTML/CSS/JS, no build step, works offline. Shared design system in [`assets/`](assets/)
(`styles.css` + `widgets.js`); interactive widgets (quizzes, calculators, simulators, fault-finders) are
self-contained per lesson.

## Note

This is a personal course written for one learner; the examples use that person's stats and a wrist (scaphoid)
constraint. Treat it as educational, not medical advice — clear new training with a doctor.
