# Added the Fuel & Recover phase (L8–L10), creatine as centrepiece

Session 4 (2026-06-28). Chris asked for three more lessons, "one including the effects of creatine." Built the
whole Phase-3 Fuel & Recover block (researched fresh via two sub-agents, then authored), each with a custom widget:

- **L8 · Protein Without Obsession** (file 0005) — ~1.6–2.2 g/kg of goal weight → ~180–200 g/day; ~0.4 g/kg per
  meal but total dominates; quality; kidney & anabolic-window myths. Interactive: protein-target calculator
  (goal weight × phase × meals → per-meal plates with leucine ✓).
- **L9 · Creatine & the Evidence-Based Shelf** (file 0006) — the centrepiece. Mechanism (PCr/ATP, an amplifier not
  a builder), ~+1 kg lean mass, intracellular water, 3–5 g/day monohydrate, loading optional, kidney-myth debunk,
  responders/vegetarians/older adults, emerging cognition. Two interactives: a saturation scrubber (loading vs
  3–5 g/day → same plateau by ~day 28) and a sort-the-shelf game (worth-it / situational / skip with verdicts).
- **L10 · Sleep, Recovery & the Small Deficit** (file 0007) — stimulus–recovery–adaptation; sleep deprivation
  (MPS −18%, T −24%, cortisol +21%; short-sleep dieting loses muscle not fat); recomp on a ~300–500 kcal deficit
  at 0.5–0.7%/wk; cut-now-lean-bulk-later. Interactive: recomp simulator (deficit + sleep + protein + RT → weekly
  fat loss, muscle change, retention score) — drop sleep below 6 h and the lean-loss flag fires (Nedeltcheva).
